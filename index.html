Welcome to the DMV Driver's License Test

The test consists of 30 multiple choice questions. You will have a maximum of 30 seconds for each question. You are required to answer 24 questions correctly. If you miss 6 questions during the duration of the test, you will automatically fail. As soon as you answer 24 questions correctly, you will pass the test. Please contact your nearest DMV associate for further instructions.
